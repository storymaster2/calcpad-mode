    Steel¶

    CalcpadCE worksheets in this section compute the geometric properties of doubly-symmetric I-sections — area, centroid, second moment of area, plastic and elastic section moduli, shear area and torsional constants — that feed directly into Eurocode EN 1993-1-1 verifications for cross-section resistance and stability. The section data can be entered manually, picked from a drop-down list of rolled IPE, IPN, HE and HD profiles, or read from a shared Excel database, so the same calculation engine serves quick checks and full design reports.

    The direct entry sheet starts from the seven dimensions \(h\), \(b_{f1}\), \(b_{f2}\), \(t_w\), \(t_{f1}\), \(t_{f2}\), \(r\) and produces the full set of cross-section properties for an asymmetric or welded build-up I-shape. The drop-down catalogue variant lets the user pick a rolled section from a single list, automatically loads the dimensions, and renders an SVG drawing of the cross-section alongside the numerical results. The Excel-driven version reads the dimensions from Steel Sections EN.xlsx via the read directive and selects the row by an integer identifier, which keeps the worksheet small and the database editable.

    A small SVG drawing library, svg_drawing.cpd, is included by the drawing variant to sketch the I-section with its flange widths, web thickness and root radius.

    I-Section Properties¶

    Cross-section properties of an asymmetric or built-up I-section computed from seven user-supplied dimensions, with classification per Eurocode EN 1993-1-1 and elastic and plastic section moduli for both axes.

    I-Section Properties Excel¶

    Excel-driven variant: the rolled-section dimensions are read from Steel Sections EN.xlsx by the read directive, selected by an integer identifier that maps to a row in the IPE or HE database.

    I-Section Properties and Drawing¶

    Catalogue-driven variant of the I-section properties calculation: a single drop-down selector populates the dimensions and an SVG sketch of the chosen rolled section is rendered alongside the results.
